Our next monthly walk is in

Blockley

Cotswalds

Gloucestershire

Sunday 1st January 2026

 

 

Go to the News page for all the information and click on the route map below to see where you can walk – plan your day!

We travel by coach every month to walk in hilly locations such as the Peak District and the Cotswolds, sometimes a bit further afield.  There is always a choice of led walks, of varying length and amount of climbing, with everyone meeting up at the end to enjoy a refreshing drink before travelling back to Leicester.

Two or three times a year weekend trips are arranged to more distant locations such as Wales and the Lake District.

To contact us please email the membership secretary via the link on the club information page.
