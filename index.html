Every month we set off by coach to explore some of the country’s most beautiful hillwalking areas, including the Peak District and the Cotswolds – and occasionally even further afield. With a choice of guided walks to suit different distances and abilities, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. And at the end of each walk, we all come together to relax with a well-earned drink before heading back to Leicester.

Occasionally, we also arrange weekend and longer trips to Wales and the Lake District. We also ventured abroad a few years ago to the Italian Dolomites.
