Our next monthly walk

Blockley, Cotswolds

Sunday 1st January 2026

We travel by coach every month to walk in hilly locations such as the Peak District and the Cotswolds, sometimes further afield.

There is always a choice of led walks of varying length and difficulty, with everyone meeting at the end for a drink before returning to Leicester.

Two or three times a year, weekend trips are arranged to locations such as Wales and the Lake District.
